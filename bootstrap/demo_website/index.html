For instructors

Save Time

  • Create courses/digital curricula and assemble content with drag-and-drop ease
  • Update courses and content anytime, anywhere
  • Assemble only once: it all remains available to you from year to year

Engage your students

  • Give students up-to-date, affordable course materials
  • Integrate multimedia with open textbooks or proprietary textbooks, sold on a chapter-by-chapter basis
  • Annotate and highlight PDFs
  • Offer your courses to students in an interactive CoursePilot™
  • Keep track of student engagement through course analytics

For students

Study efficiently

  • Access open courses and content for free in an easy-to-use dashboard
  • Pay for your textbooks as you go, on a chapter-by-chapter basis
  • Upload and manage learning content in your personal library
  • Annotate and highlight PDFs
  • Keep your dashboard and resources with you from year to year, for free

Connect through learning

  • Experience learning in an interactive CoursePilot™ for a small fee
  • Shape your CoursePilot™ according to the way you learn best
  • Fully experience multimedia resources without leaving your CoursePilot™
  • Share resources you create with students around the world
