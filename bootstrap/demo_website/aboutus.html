About us. Who we are

Open Assembly

Open Assembly is an education technology company lowering the cost of higher education for students by giving educators tools to assemble digital curricula with affordable course materials, open and proprietary.

Open Assembly is an open, cloud-based platform providing a new kind of curricular resource strategy for hybrid and online learning environments. It allows educators to easily and quickly create learning playlists using any combination of digital media. Students experience and engage these learning playlists in an interactive personal learning environment (PLE).

  • a simple, intuitive, drag-and-drop interface
  • an interactive, personal learning environment (CoursePilot™)
